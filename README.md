## 📌 Project Overview

The **Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using **Power BI** and **Microsoft Excel** that transforms raw financial sales data into interactive, insight-driven dashboards.

The system analyzes:

`Sales Performance` · `Profitability Trends` · `Discount Impact` · `Customer Segments` · `Country-wise Performance` · `Product Profitability`

> Developed as part of the **130-Hour "Data Analytics, Cloud Computing & AI"** Training Program organized by **Godrej & Eduramp**.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Most businesses struggle to answer critical questions like:

| Business Challenge | This Dashboard Solves It By |
|---|---|
| Which segment drives the most profit? | Segment-wise profit distribution analysis |
| How do discounts affect margins? | Discount band vs profitability comparison |
| Which products perform best? | Product-wise KPI tracking |
| Which country leads in revenue? | Country-wise sales & profit breakdown |
| Are we making data-driven decisions? | Centralized KPI dashboard |

## 🚀 Project Modules

### 📊 Module 1 — Sales Analysis

> **Objective:** Analyze overall sales performance across products and customer segments.

| Finding | Insight |
|---|---|
| 🏆 Top Product | Paseo generated the highest sales among all products |
| 📊 Segment Variation | Sales performance varied significantly across customer segments |

---

### 💰 Module 2 — Profitability Analysis

> **Objective:** Identify the most profitable business segments and products.

| Finding | Insight |
|---|---|
| 🏛️ Top Segment | Government segment contributed the highest overall profit |
| 📦 Best Product | Paseo emerged as the strongest-performing product |

---

### 🎯 Module 3 — Discount Impact Analysis

> **Objective:** Analyze how discount strategies affect profitability.

| Finding | Insight |
|---|---|
| ✅ Optimal Strategy | Low and Medium discounts generated the highest profit margins |
| ⚠️ Risk Zone | Heavy discounting significantly reduced overall profitability |

---

### 🌍 Module 4 — Country-wise Performance Analysis

> **Objective:** Analyze sales and profitability distribution across markets.

| Country | Performance |
|---|---|
| 🥇 France | Highest profit share across all markets |
| 🌍 Germany & Canada | Significant contributors to overall revenue |
| 🌱 USA & Mexico | Identified as high-potential future growth markets |

---

### 👥 Module 5 — Customer Segment Analysis

> **Objective:** Understand how each customer segment contributes to sales and profit.

| Segment | Contribution |
|---|---|
| 🏆 Government | Highest sales and profit across all products |
| 📉 Enterprise & Channel Partners | Comparatively lower profit contribution |

---

## 🧠 Business Intelligence Insights

Key strategic findings from the dashboard:

- 🏛️ **Government segment** is the primary revenue and profit driver
- 💰 **Balanced discount strategies** are directly linked to higher profitability
- 📦 **Product performance** — particularly Paseo — significantly impacts business growth
- 🌍 **Country-wise analysis** reveals France as the top market and USA/Mexico as expansion targets
- 📊 **KPI dashboards** translate complex data into clear strategic decisions
